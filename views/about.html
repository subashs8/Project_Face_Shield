How We Started

Healthcare workers like my parents are helping and battling out in the frontlines during the COVID-19 outbreak. Not to mention there are several other employees working in various different industries whose health needs to be cared for. However, there is a shortage of masks and other essential items. To ensure their protection we are producing face shields.

End of the day, we are just a bunch of highschoolers who are trying to utilize our creativity and spirit to contribute to the world!
